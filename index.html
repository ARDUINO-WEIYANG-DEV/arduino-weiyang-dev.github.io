Which board to choose?

  • 🔹 Arduino UNO – Perfect for beginners, simple projects, and learning electronics.
  • 🔹 ESP32 – Built-in Wi-Fi & Bluetooth, ideal for IoT and advanced wireless projects.
  • 🔹 ESP8266 – Low-cost Wi-Fi module, great for lightweight IoT devices and home automation.

Featured Projects

Smart Plant Watering System

Use an Arduino UNO with a soil moisture sensor and a small water pump to automatically water your plants when the soil gets dry. This project is perfect for beginners and helps you understand analog readings and motor control.

ESP32 Weather Station

Connect an ESP32 to a DHT22 sensor and an OLED display to show temperature and humidity. Send data to the cloud (e.g., ThingSpeak) and monitor your room's climate from anywhere. A great introduction to IoT and wireless communication.

ESP8266 Home Light Controller

Control your home lights via a web interface or smartphone app using an ESP8266 and relays. Learn about MQTT, HTTP requests, and how to build a simple home automation system that runs locally or over the internet.
